About Us

The Product & Consulting Club at MANIT Bhopal, widely known as ProdCon, is a premier student-led initiative committed to nurturing future leaders in the fields of Consulting and Product Management. We aim not only to create but also to foster a high-impact learning ecosystem where students gain real-world exposure and develop structured business problem-solving skills.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students from diverse fields, the Product & Consultancy Club aims to foster innovation, critical thinking, and real-world problem-solving. We believe that great ideas deserve structure — and that's where product thinking meets consulting strategy.

Learn More

What We Do

Our activities revolve around hands-on learning and real-world simulations. From product design sprints and user research to business case consulting and industry collaborations, our members get exposure to a wide spectrum of problem-solving challenges.

Learn More

Our Projects & Impact

In just a short time, our members have worked on exciting campus and local community projects. From improving student service processes to building productivity tools for campus life, we're already making an impact.
